Our Goals

HRF

CORE VALUES(CAEEC)

1. Compassion

We prioritize empathy, kindness, and understanding in all our interactions, recognizing the physical and emotional challenges faced by individuals affected by Congenital heart disease.

2. Accessibility

We strive to make healthcare and resources more accessible and inclusive for all, regardless of their background, financial situation, or circumstances.

3. Excellence

We commit to delivering exceptional support, services, and resources, continuously improving our processes and programs to achieve outstanding outcomes.

4. Empowerment

We empower individuals and families affected by congenital heart disease with knowledge, resources, and support, enabling them to take control of their health, well-being, and futures.

5. Collaboration

We foster partnerships, collaborations, and connections with healthcare providers, organizations, and communities to amplify our impact, leverage resources, and drive positive change.
