Gilbert Kiakia Goodnews

Program Founder / Officers head

Gilbert, Kiaka Goodnews is a visionary creator, communicator, innovator and a passionate humanitarian worker who has distinguished himself in national development through community service. He has several awards to his name, notably are Presidential award, Rivers and Bayelsa State Honours award, Role Model in National Service from Rivers ministry of Youth Development, and Ogoni personality of the year (2021). He currently work as an information officer in the federal ministry of information and National Orientation. He holds a BA in English and Communications Art from IAUE, Rivers State.
