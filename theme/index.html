About us

Join the Movement to Heal Hearts and Transform Lives

At Heart Recovery Foundation (HRF), we're driven by a profound passion to provide exceptional care and support to heart patients and their loved ones. Our mission is to bridge the gap in healthcare access, education, and research, empowering individuals and communities to thrive. Your generous support enables us to: Fund life-changing heart surgeries and interventions for those in need. Advance groundbreaking cardiovascular research and education, driving innovation and improvement in patient outcomes. Empower communities to prevent heart diseases and promote healthy lifestyles through awareness campaigns, education, and outreach programs. By joining our movement, you'll be part of a community that's dedicated to: Saving lives through timely interventions and surgeries. Improving quality of life for heart patients and their families. Fostering a culture of compassion, empathy, and kindness. Together, let's unite to heal hearts, transform lives, and create a brighter future for all.
