Mr. Sunday Adejoh Baba, fildc, fnipr

Board of Trustees

Sunday Adejoh Baba, fildc, fnipr, is a distinguished public servant, scholar, and communication expert. He holds a B.A. (Hons) in English from Bayero University, Kano, an M.A. in English, and a Ph.D. in New Media Research. Joining the Federal Civil Service in 1992, he rose to the position of Director in 2017; holding various key roles, including CEO of the National Institute of Public Information, Acting General Manager of the National Theatre, and Director of Public Communications and National Orientation. An accomplished trainer and speaker, Sunday Adejoh developed and taught courses on New Media, Information Management, and Leadership, and has delivered numerous papers on Public Relations and Crisis Management. He is a Fellow of the Nigerian Institute of Public Relations and the Institute of Leadership and Development Communication. Following his retirement in 2024, he was appointed Special Assistant (Administration) to the Hon. Minister of Information and National Orientation.

Mr. Sunday Adejoh Baba, fildc, fnipr

Malam Suleiman Haruna (PhD)

Board of Trustees

Suleiman Haruna is a versatile communicator, writer, and teacher who has worked with experienced and motivated experts and has grown to be a reference point and a candidate of choice whenever a good hand is required for service. He has also managed staff for over 20 years as Head of Information and Communication in various organizations, including a World Bank-assisted social investment programme, YESSO, A special intervention programme, SURE-P, and the Presidential Villa, working directly with the First Lady of Nigeria for seven years as Director of Information. He is currently Director of Public Relations at the Federal Ministry of Information. He holds a PhD in Development Communication from Ahmadu Bello University, Zaria.

Dr. Adeleke A. Adeoye

Board of Trustees

Dr. Adeleke A. Adeoye is a dedicated General Medical Practitioner and Public Health Physician who has distinguished himself in the service to his patients and humanity. He obtained his primary medical qualification form Obafemi Awolowo University, Ile-ife in 2015 and has continuously offered his expertise to humanity since then. He currently works as a Medical Officer in the Federal Ministry of Health and Social Welfare while also undergoing further fellowship training in General Practice in Adelaide, South Australia.

Dr. James Nda ABALAKA JP, PhD, FCNA, FCTI, MNIM, ani

Board of Trustees

Dr. James Nda ABALAKA JP, PhD, FCNA, FCTI, MNIM, ani Dr. James Nda Abalaka is a renowned Civil Servant, Scholar, teacher, and a financial/management expert. He attended the Prestigious University of Jos, Plateau State where he bagged his first degree, a B.Sc. in Business Management in 1989. He also holds a Master in Business Administration (MBA) in Finance & Investment from ABU Zaria; an M.Sc. and a doctorate degree (PhD) in Management from the Kogi State University, Anyigba. In addition, he holds a Professional Diploma in Education. He is presently a doctoral student at the Federal University Lokoja where he is carrying out a research work on an Appraisal of Public Financial Management Reforms in Public Expenditure in Nigeria’s Treasury from 2011-2023. He joined the Federal Civil Service as a Finance Officer II in the early 90s with the Federal Ministry of Water Resources and rose through the Professional Accountant cadre in his blossoming career spanning over three (3) decades. He was at a time, the Head of Internal Audit Department at the State House, Presidential Villa, Abuja, where his dutifulness speaks volumes till date. He presently the Director, Funds Department in the Office of the Accountant General of the Federation (OAGF), Treasury House, Abuja. A key reformist and major role player in the Public Financial Management Reforms of the Treasury, Dr. Abalaka has served in several capacities in the country’s Public Service> He is the current Chairman of the Implementation Committee of the Revised Bottom-up Cash Management Policy of the Federal Government of Nigeria; Member, FAAC IPSAS Implementation Committee; Member/Secretary, Inter-Ministerial Committee on TSA Implementation; Member/Secretary, Ways and Means Reconciliation Committee; National Coordinator, Bottom-up Cash Planning of the FGN on the GIFMIS platform; Coordinator, National Treasury Workshops, amongst others. Professional Certification and Membership He is a Member of various professional and intellectual bodies across the globe, including but not limited to: Member, Policy, Leadership and Strategic Course 38 (PLSC 38, ani) of the National Institute of Strategic Studies, Kuru,-Jos Fellow of the Association of National Accountants of Nigeria (FCNA); Fellow, Chartered Institute of Taxation (FCTI) Fellow, Chartered Institute of Cost & Management Accountants (FCIMA) Member, Nigerian Institute of Management (NIM) Fellow, Institute of Certified Public Accountants (FCPA) Fellow, Institute of Chartered Economics of Nigeria (FCE) Fellow, Chartered Institute of Forensic and Investigative Auditors of Nigeria Member, Institute of Public Administration of Nigeria (APA) Member, National Institute of Marketing of Nigeria (MNIMN) Fellow: International Chartered World Learned Society (FICWLS) since 2020 Fellow: Chartered World Institute of Encyclopaedia of Books (FCWEB) Since 2020 Academic Works and Publications He has several over sixty (60) scholarly works and publications to his credit in both local and international journals. He has attended and anchored several local and international Courses, Workshops, Seminars and Retreats and presented papers at some of the for a, including several study tours to Philippines, Malaysia, The Gambia, Ghana and also gone on several missions to Papua New Guinea in the Pacific, South Africa, Botswana, Israel and a host of others.

Dr. James Nda ABALAKA JP, PhD, FCNA, FCTI, MNIM, ani
